## Career Narrative Story Formats

Research basis for the STAR, ATOLA, and Personal story formats defined in `rules/career_narratives/`.

**STAR Format**
- Lore, Richard. *Behavioral Interviewing: The STAR Technique.* Widely attributed to the DDI (Development Dimensions International) behavioral interviewing methodology, 1970s.
- The Balance Careers — *How to Use the STAR Interview Response Technique*
- Indeed Career Guide — *How to Use the STAR Method for Your Next Job Interview*
- Harvard Business Review — *How to Answer Behavioral Interview Questions*

**ATOLA Format**
- AstraZeneca and similar global pharma organizations use ATOLA (Actions, Thinking, Outcomes, Learnings, Applications) as a structured behavioral interview framework emphasizing learning agility and reasoning process over action sequence.
- Reference derived from practitioner experience; no single published source. Confirmed as an active format in global pharma hiring contexts.

## Career Narrative Decision Formats

Research basis for the Personal and ADR decision formats defined in `rules/career_narratives/`.

**ADR (Architecture Decision Record)**
- Nygard, Michael. *Documenting Architecture Decisions.* Cognitect, 2011. Original description of the ADR format. thinkrelevance.com/blog/2011/11/15/documenting-architecture-decisions
- AWS Prescriptive Guidance. *Architecture Decision Records.* docs.aws.amazon.com/prescriptive-guidance/latest/architectural-decision-records
- Microsoft Azure Well-Architected Framework. *Architecture Decision Records.* learn.microsoft.com/azure/well-architected/architect-role/architecture-decision-record
- ADR GitHub. *ADR Templates and Tools.* adr.github.io

**Decision Journal (Personal Format Basis)**
- Duke, Annie. *Thinking in Bets: Making Smarter Decisions When You Don't Have All the Facts.* Portfolio/Penguin, 2018. Basis for the decision journal concept — capturing context, reasoning, and predicted outcomes at time of decision for retrospective review.
- Parrish, Shane. *The Decision Journal.* Farnam Street (fs.blog). Practitioner adaptation of the decision journal format for ongoing personal use.
