# CV Generation QC Checklist

> Load this file at the QC phase following CV content generation. Apply the Universal section first against all generated content. Then apply only the archetype section matching the active archetype and confirmed role level. For each item, cite specific evidence from the generated CV content — a specific bullet, section, or line. General confirmations are not acceptable. State Pass or Fail for each item. Any Fail invokes QC Failure Recovery before this phase can close.

---

## Universal — All Archetypes and Role Levels

**U1 — Bullet length audit**
Review every bullet in the Professional Experience section. Every bullet must be 2 lines or fewer. Any bullet that is 3 lines must be listed here with the role block it appears in and explicit justification for why it qualifies as a high-complexity exception. No bullet may exceed 3 lines — split or compress any that do before proceeding.

**U2 — Em-dash prohibition**
Confirm no bullet or prose uses an em-dash as a clause connector. List any violations. Em-dashes in company/location header lines are the only permitted exception.

**U3 — Achievement opening words**
Review the first word or phrase of every bullet. No achievement may open with a tool name, programming language, or technical method. Cite the opening word of any bullet where this is borderline and confirm compliance.

**U4 — Summary required elements**
Confirm the professional summary contains all four required elements defined in the active archetype. State each element and cite where it appears in the summary.

**U5 — Section order**
List the actual section order in the generated CV and confirm it matches the required order from the active archetype.

**U6 — Technical proficiencies position**
Confirm the Technical Proficiencies section is the last section in the document.

**U7 — Selected Projects section**
Check the Independent & Volunteer Projects section of the Experience Inventory used in Phase 3a. If entries exist there, confirm a Selected Projects section is present in the generated CV, positioned as section 4 per the active archetype. If no entries exist, confirm the Selected Projects section is absent. A present-but-empty section or a missing section when entries exist are both failures — cite the inventory status and CV structure to confirm.

## Archetype 1 Leadership: Transformation & Strategy

**A1L-1 — Summary framing**
Confirm the summary leads with transformation and capability-building expertise. Confirm it does not lead with process optimization, technical capabilities, or steady-state operational management. Cite the opening of the summary.

**A1L-2 — Greenfield or turnaround achievements**
Confirm at least two achievements describe a new organizational capability, operating model, or structural change built from greenfield or turnaround context. Cite each qualifying achievement.

**A1L-3 — Adoption or scale outcomes**
Confirm at least two achievements include an adoption or scale outcome — number of users, teams, regions, programs, or adoption rate. Cite each qualifying achievement and the specific metric or scale indicator present.

**A1L-4 — Stakeholder navigation**
Confirm stakeholder navigation, resistance management, or organizational complexity is explicitly described in at least one achievement. Cite the specific language used.

**A1L-5 — Transformation achievement elements**
Confirm the four transformation elements (challenge, strategy, execution, outcome) are present within at least two BioMarin achievements. Cite each achievement and identify where each element appears within the bullet.
